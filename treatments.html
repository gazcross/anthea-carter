Treatments

Find out about the treatments I offer and the benefits they provide to your wellbeing

Reiki

Reiki is an ancient and simple Japanese healing technique using natural healing energy to restore balance and encourages the body's natural ability to heal itself. You will remain fully clothed laying comfortably on the massage couch or seated on a chair where a series on non-invasive hand positions are used. If a massage has already been received, you will remain covered with towels.

Reiki has many benefits which include:

  • Deep relaxation and helps the body to release anxiety, stress and tension
  • Aids better sleep
  • Pain relief and supports the body's healing process
  • The ability to cope with difficult situations
  • Help to improve overall wellbeing.
  • Relief during emotional turmoil.

Oriental Face Massage

This is a wonderfully relaxing treatment available to people of all ages, helping stress and tension disappear! It is an effective non-surgical treatment to reduce the signs of ageing and help to rejuvenate the skin. It is derived from various face massages and acupressure techniques performed by the Chinese, Japanese, Indians and Tibetans since ancient times. This treatment works on the shoulders, neck, scalp and mostly the face where grapeseed oil is used with the client relaxing on the comfortable massage couch.

The benefits of the Oriental Face Massage can also help to:

  • Relieve pain and stiffness in the muscles of the targeted area.
  • Alleviate stress, anxiety, lethargy and mild depression
  • Relieve tension headaches, eye strain, nasal congestion and aching jaws
  • Improve blood circulation and lymphatic drainage
  • Increase elasticity in the skin
  • Invigorate and strengthen muscles in the face to tighten saggy, puffy skin.

Swedish Body Massage

Allow your stress and tension to float away as you lay back and relax while enjoying a series of massage techniques used on the back, legs, feet, arms and hands in which grapeseed oil is used. Towels will be used at all times to cover areas not being worked on, to keep you warm and comfortable. Body massage has been used for thousands of years as a simple and effective method of attaining and maintaining good health and its benefits have long been recognised in many cultures throughout the world.

Benefits of the Swedish Body Massage include:

  • Releasing emotional and mental tension
  • Reducing pain and tension as the body releases endorphins
  • Creating a feeling of wellbeing
  • Improving mobility and flexibility
  • Improving sleep
  • Supporting healthy immunity and improving blood circulation.

Indian Head Massage

Unwind during this wonderful treatment which helps to both relax and re-energize you. This is a seated treatment where a variety of massage movements are used, working on the neck, shoulders, arms, head and face, in which grapeseed oil is applied to the skin. Indian Head Massage is based on the Ayurvedic system of healing which has been practised in India for thousands of years. The therapeutic effects last long after the treatment is over.

The benefits include many of the following:

  • Relief from pain and stiffness in the muscles of the targeted area.
  • Increased mobility of the joints in the neck and shoulder area.
  • Relieves symptoms of stress, anxiety and mild depression.
  • Improved quality of sleep
  • Relief from tension headaches, eye strain, nasal congestion and aching jaw.
  • Increased self-awareness, energy and concentration levels.
